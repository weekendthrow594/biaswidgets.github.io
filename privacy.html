Bias Widgets — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Bias Widgets mobile application (hereafter "the App") developed by Li (hereafter "we", "us", or "the Service Provider"). The App is offered as a freemium service for iOS devices and is provided "as is".

This document explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, the following technical information may be collected automatically by the App or by third-party services we integrate (see Section 3):

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and language settings
  • App usage events (such as which screens you visit, the time and duration of your visits, and which features you interact with)
  • Anonymous device identifiers used for analytics and advertising attribution (such as Apple's IDFA, IDFV, and SKAdNetwork postbacks)
  • Approximate IP address (used to estimate region; we do not store precise location)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data

The App does not collect precise GPS location information.

1.2 Information You Provide

The App allows you to personalize your widgets by entering content such as your bias's name, dates, photos, and quotes. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework and is shared with the App's widget extension via an Apple App Group container. We do not upload this user-provided content to our servers.

1.3 Subscription & Purchase Information

If you purchase a Widget+ subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We receive an anonymous transaction receipt to verify your subscription status. We do not receive your name, email address, payment card information, or Apple ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features
  • To improve performance, fix bugs, and develop new functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns through aggregated and anonymized data
  • To display relevant advertising (see Section 4)
  • To verify subscription status and entitlements
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle the data they collect:

Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Firestore to understand how users interact with the App and to deliver remote configuration. Firebase may collect anonymous identifiers, device information, and usage events.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google AdMob (Google LLC)

We use Google AdMob to display advertising within the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA, where permitted), usage data, and approximate location to deliver relevant ads.

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Apple StoreKit (Apple Inc.)

StoreKit handles all in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not receive any payment details.

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Advertising & Tracking Transparency (ATT)

If you are using iOS 14.5 or later, the App will display Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before any third-party tracking takes place. You may grant or deny tracking permission at any time:

  1. Open the iOS Settings app
  2. Tap Privacy & Security → Tracking
  3. Toggle Bias Widgets on or off

If you deny tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be less relevant to your interests. We respect your choice and will not attempt to track you across other apps and websites without your consent.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the third-party service providers listed in Section 3, who process data on our behalf and are bound by their respective privacy policies
  • To comply with applicable law, legal process, or a valid government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Service Provider, our users, or the public, or to investigate and prevent fraud or abuse
  • In aggregated or anonymized form that cannot reasonably be used to identify you

6. Data Retention

Content you create within the App (bias names, dates, photos, quotes) is stored locally on your device for as long as you keep the App installed. Uninstalling the App permanently removes this data from your device.

Anonymous analytics and crash logs collected by Firebase and AdMob are retained according to those services' retention policies (typically up to 14 months for Firebase Analytics).

If you would like us to assist with deletion of any data you believe we hold about you, please contact us at the email address listed in Section 11.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EU, UK, California), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your data
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

You can also stop all data collection by the App at any time by uninstalling it. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address listed in Section 11.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. In jurisdictions where the minimum age is higher (such as 16 in parts of the EU), the same protections apply.

If you believe that a child under the applicable minimum age has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action, including deleting that information.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information we process. Sensitive content you create is stored locally in the iOS sandbox and shared only with the App's widget extension via Apple's App Group mechanism. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you within the App. Your continued use of the App after a revised policy takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
