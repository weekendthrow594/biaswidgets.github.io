Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?

  1. Long-press an empty area on your Home Screen until the apps jiggle.
  2. Tap the "+" icon in the top-left corner.
  3. Search for "Bias Widgets" and select the size you want.
  4. Tap "Add Widget" and place it where you'd like.

How do I add a widget to my Lock Screen?

  1. Long-press the Lock Screen and tap "Customize".
  2. Tap the area below the clock, then tap "+ Add Widgets".
  3. Select Bias Widgets from the list.

My widget isn't updating. What should I do?

iOS controls when widgets refresh. To force a refresh, try:

  • Tap the widget to open the app, then return to the Home Screen.
  • Restart your device.
  • Remove the widget and add it again.

I purchased Widget+ on one device. How do I restore it on another?

Open Bias Widgets → Settings → "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my Widget+ subscription?

  1. Open the iOS Settings app.
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top.
  3. Tap "Subscriptions".
  4. Find "Bias Widget+" and tap "Cancel Subscription".
